PREVENTION

Specific recommendations for the prevention of acneiform rash:

Starting from the first cetuximab dose basic care measures can reduce the severity and extent of skin reactions. The following specific recommendations for prophylaxis are based primarily on results from clinical trials:
CARE

• Use Fragrance-free, moisturizing creams or lotions

CLEAN

• Wash with tepid warm water
• Use of soap-free, pH5-neutral bath shower oils
SUN SCREEN

Sunscreen with a sun protection factor ≥ 25
(at least 30 min before sun exposure)

ANTIBIOTICS-PROPHYLAXIS

• Oral administration of antibiotics*:
• Doxycycline ** 100 mg - 200 mg / day ≥ 8 weeks
• Minocycline 100 mg/day ≥ 8 weeks
