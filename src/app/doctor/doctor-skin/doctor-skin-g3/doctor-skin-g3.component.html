Papulo Pustular Eruption
Grade 3

SKIN(1-10)... Sites of eruption: For Face, Scalp, Chest or Back
  • Clinically
  • Management

> 20 Papules and/or Pustules or > 5 areas of erythema and odema +/- itching and pain or affecting emotions and functions
N.B. NCI-CT grading includes a stage 4 designated as Exfoliative, bullous or ulcerating dermatitis “Lyell-like”

Management of grade 2 & 3:(1-7)
Doxycycline 100-200 mg/day caps + Vitamin K cream containing 0.1% 2 × daily and other topicals as before


No response after 4 weeks

Add prednisone 5 mg tabs (0.5 mg/kg) for 10 days


No response after 10 days

Add Isotretinion caps 10 mg/day or 0.3-0.5 mg/kg/day (check serum cholesterol & triglycerides every 2 weeks and signs of Xerosis. Caution should be taken if patient has pyogenic granuloma on digits)


No response after 4 weeks

Culture from pustular lesions if present & treat accordingly or refer


Grade 3 no response

Interrupt anti-EGFR treatment for 2 weeks till rash downgrades
