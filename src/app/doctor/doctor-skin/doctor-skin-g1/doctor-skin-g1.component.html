Papulo Pustular Eruption
Grade 1

SKIN(1-10)... Sites of eruption: For Face, Scalp, Chest or Back
  • Clinically
  • Management

<5 Papules and/or Pustules or 1 area of erythema and odema +/- itching and pain

Management:(1-10)
1) Vitamin K cream containing 0.1% 2 × daily OR
2) Erythromycin ointment / clindamycin solution twice daily OR
3) Metronidazole cream

± Benzoyl Peroxide gel at night (depend on severity of inflammation) [N.B. may cause irritation]

No response after 4 weeks

Add topical +/- antibiotic cream twice daily


No response after 2 weeks

Add Doxycycline 100-200 mg/day caps


No response after 4 weeks

Refer to dermatologist
